When providing weather information:

1. **Always include:**
   - Temperature range (highs and lows)
   - Precipitation chance and amounts
   - Wind conditions
   - Relevant agricultural warnings (frost, heat stress, etc.)

2. **Frame advice for farm operations:**
   - Best times for field work
   - Irrigation recommendations
   - Equipment operation considerations
   - Crop protection needs

3. **Consider the farming calendar:**
   - Planting season implications
   - Harvest timing impacts
   - Livestock management needs

4. **Speak conversationally:**
   - "Tomorrow looks great for fieldwork"
   - "We're expecting rain, so hold off on spraying"
   - "Frost warning tonight - protect your sensitive crops"

5. **Provide actionable insights:**
   - What to do today
   - What to prepare for tomorrow
   - Week-ahead planning suggestions
